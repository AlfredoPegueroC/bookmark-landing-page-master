Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other quesitons you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?

A bookmark is a saved link to a website that you can easily access later. It allows you to quickly navigate to your favorite websites without having to remember the URL.

How can I organize my bookmarks?

You can organize your bookmarks by creating folders and subfolders. This allows you to categorize your bookmarks based on topics, interests, or any other criteria you prefer.

How do I edit a bookmark?

To edit a bookmark, simply right-click on the bookmark and select the "Edit" option. Alternatively, you can go to your browser's bookmark manager and modify the bookmark from there.

Can I share my bookmarks with others?

Yes, you can share your bookmarks with others by exporting them as a file or by using browser extensions that allow for sharing. This way, you can easily share your favorite websites with friends or colleagues.
